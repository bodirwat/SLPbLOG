Canon EOS R6 Mirrorless Camera


The Canon EOS R6 is a versatile mirrorless camera designed for enthusiasts and professionals alike, offering impressive performance and advanced features. With a 20.1-megapixel full-frame CMOS sensor and DIGIC X image processor, the R6 delivers stunning image quality and high-speed performance. Key Features: 20.1MP Full-Frame CMOS Sensor: Capture sharp, detailed images with excellent dynamic range, even in low light conditions. In-Body Image Stabilization (IBIS): Enjoy up to 8 stops of shake correction for sharper handheld shooting and smoother video footage. Dual Pixel CMOS AF II: Experience fast and accurate autofocus performance with 1053 selectable points covering approximately 100% of the frame. 4K UHD and Full HD Video Recording: Shoot cinematic 4K video at up to 60fps or Full HD at up to 120fps for stunning slow-motion effects.
